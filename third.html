7 places to check out when visiting St Louis (that aren’t the Arch)

The Gateway to the West! St. Louis is a city with various things to do, aside from the famous St. Louis Arch. We’ve compiled a list of things to do, indoors and outdoors, on your next adventure to the city.

Busch Stadium

700 Clark Ave.
St. Louis, MO
63102

Visit MLB's website

Home of the St. Louis Cardinals! Catch a game during the baseball season, or book a tour of the stadium.

The City Museum

750 N 16th St.
St. Louis, MO
63103

Visit the City Museum website

A “Weirdly Wonderful” experience for all ages. This art-based playground is a unique place that gives ‘fun’ a new meaning!

The St. Louis Zoo

1 Government Dr.
St. Louis, MO
63103

Visit the St. Louis Zoo's website

Explore over 600 different species of animals through visual or interactive experiences at the Zoo.

The Fox Theater

527 N Grand Blvd.
St. Louis, MO
63103

Visit The Fabulous Fox's website

This historical theater offers a luxurious lounge space between your next tour, or perhaps a show!

Union Station

1820 Market Street
St. Louis, MO
63103

Visit the Union Station website

Dedicate your trip to exploring the Union Station! Offering attractions such as The Aquarium, The STL Wheel, a variety of food options, and more!

Lemp Mansion

3322 Demenil Pl.
St. Louis, MO
63118

Visit Lemp Mansion's website

As soon on Ghost Adventures, The Lemp Mansion comes with a “Haunted History,” where you can go for dinner, an overnight stay, or a spooky tour.

Anheuser Busch Brewery

1200 Lynch St
St. Louis, MO
63118
